# Personal OS

> **An Open Source AI Operating System for Personal Decision Intelligence**

---

# Vision

Most software helps people **do things**.

Personal OS exists to help people **make better decisions**.

Instead of building another chatbot, note-taking application, expense tracker, or productivity tool, Personal OS brings together every aspect of a person's digital life into a single intelligent platform that continuously learns, adapts, and assists.

Our goal is to build an **AI Operating System** that understands context, remembers what matters, reasons across multiple domains, and provides transparent, explainable recommendations throughout a person's lifetime.

---

# Mission

Build an open, modular, extensible platform that combines:

* Long-term Memory
* Decision Intelligence
* Event-Driven Architecture
* AI Agents
* Knowledge Management
* Workflow Automation
* Personal Data Ownership

into one cohesive operating system.

---

# Why Personal OS?

Today's digital ecosystem is fragmented.

One application manages finances.

Another manages calendars.

Another stores notes.

Another tracks habits.

Another answers questions.

None of them truly understand the person using them.

Personal OS unifies these experiences into a single platform that continuously builds context over time.

---

# Core Principles

## User First

The platform exists to help users make better decisions.

Not to maximize engagement.

---

## Privacy First

Users own their data.

Whenever possible:

* Local-first
* Open standards
* Exportable
* Transparent

---

## Explainability

Every recommendation should answer:

* Why?
* Why not?
* What evidence?
* Which memories?
* What assumptions?

---

## Extensibility

Everything should be replaceable.

New capabilities should be added through extension rather than modification.

---

## Open Source

Personal OS is designed to become a community-driven platform built on open standards and open technologies.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Personal OS should eventually support:

* Personal Finance
* Health
* Goals
* Calendar
* Learning
* Knowledge Management
* Career Planning
* Travel Planning
* Home Automation
* Smart Devices
* Family Collaboration

while presenting a unified understanding of the user.

---

# Success

Personal OS succeeds when it becomes a trusted companion that helps people make better decisions—not by replacing human judgment, but by augmenting it with context, memory, and explainable AI.

> **Build technology that remembers what matters, respects user ownership, and helps people make better decisions over a lifetime.**
